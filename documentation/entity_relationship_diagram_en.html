Data Design (Entity-Relationship Diagram)

Definition of the main entities of the system and their relationships.

Key Entities:

  • Student: ID, First Name, Middle Name (Optional), Paternal Surname, Maternal Surname, Preferred Surname, Nickname (Optional), Birth Date, Address, Emergency Contact, Classroom, Shift.
  • Guardian: ID, First Name, Middle Name (Optional), Paternal Surname, Maternal Surname, Preferred Surname, Relationship, Address, Phone, Email, Authorized for Pickup, Authorized for Change.
  • Staff: ID, First Name, Middle Name (Optional), Paternal Surname, Maternal Surname, Preferred Surname, Address, Phone, Email, Classroom, Role.
  • Address: ID, Street, Number, City, State, Postal Code.
  • Emergency Contact: ID, Full Name, Relationship, Phone.
  • Classroom: ID, Name, Capacity.
  • Access Level: ID, Access Name, Description.
  • Role: ID, Role Name, Access Level.
  • Attendance: ID, Student, Date, Status, Leave Type, Classroom.
  • Calendar: ID, Date, Description, Event Type, Classroom.
  • Activity: ID, Name, Description, Schedule, Teacher.
  • Conversation: ID, Classroom, Creation Date.
  • Conversation Guardian: Conversation, Guardian.
  • Conversation Staff: Conversation, Staff.
  • Guardian Message: ID, Conversation, Sender Guardian, Content, Send Date, Read.
  • Staff Message: ID, Conversation, Sender Staff, Content, Send Date, Read.

Relationships:

  • A Student has one Address, one Emergency Contact, and belongs to one Classroom.
  • A Guardian has one Address.
  • A Staff member has one Address, is assigned to one Classroom, and has one Role.
  • A Role has one Access Level.
  • Attendance records are for a Student in a Classroom on a specific Date.
  • Calendar events are associated with a Classroom.
  • Activities are in charge of a Staff member.
  • Conversations are associated with a Classroom.
  • Conversation Guardians participate in a Conversation.
  • Conversation Staff participate in a Conversation.
  • Guardian Messages are part of a Conversation and sent by a Guardian.
  • Staff Messages are part of a Conversation and sent by a Staff member.
